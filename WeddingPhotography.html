Our Workflow

The ISO Media Experience

From initial consultation to delivering your final images, we provide a seamless and enjoyable photography experience.

1

Initial Consultation

We'll meet (in person or virtually) to discuss your vision, timeline, and must-have shots. This is when we get to know each other and ensure we're the right fit.

2

Planning Session

About 2 months before your wedding, we'll create a detailed photography timeline, scout locations (if needed), and finalize all the details.

3

The Wedding Day

We arrive early to capture getting ready photos and stay through your reception. Our approach is a mix of directed portraits and documentary-style coverage.

4

Post-Production

We carefully edit each image with our signature style. You'll receive a sneak peek within 48 hours and your full gallery in 3 weeks (or less!).

5

Album Design

For packages including albums, we'll design a beautiful layout for your approval. After your review, we'll send it to our professional lab for printing.
