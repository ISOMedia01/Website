Our Workflow

The Portrait Experience

From initial consultation to delivering your final images, we provide a seamless and enjoyable photography experience.

1

Planning Consultation

We'll discuss your vision, preferred styles, locations, outfits, and goals for the session. This helps us create a customized plan tailored just for you.

2

Session Day

On the day of your session, we'll guide you through poses and expressions while keeping the atmosphere relaxed and fun. Most clients say the experience is easier and more enjoyable than they expected!

3

Image Selection

Within 1 week, you'll receive a private online gallery to review all the images from your session and select your favorites for professional editing.

4

Post-Production

We carefully edit each selected image with our signature style. You'll receive your final edited images within 2 weeks of making your selections.

5

Delivery & Products

Your final images are delivered via digital download and any ordered prints or products are shipped directly to your door.
